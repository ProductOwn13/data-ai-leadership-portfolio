Governance Model

A data mesh-aligned federated governance model using Collibra, Data Vault, shared platform capabilities and domain-owned data products.

Operating thesis

The platform team owns shared capabilities, standards, Data Vault patterns, engineering enablement and governance workflows. Domain teams own data product meaning, priorities, adoption and quality expectations.

Collibra is the system of engagement for ownership, glossary, lineage, quality, certification, policies, issue routing and AI traceability.

Minimum viable controls

  • Business owner and technical owner
  • Glossary terms and KPI definitions
  • Source-to-consumption lineage
  • Critical data elements and quality rules
  • Access policy, certification status and issue workflow
